Enterprise and Business plans drive the vast majority of our revenue and have significantly higher average CLV compared to other plans. This suggests strong product-market fit and value for larger clients.
Despite their high value, their churn rates are comparable to other plans, indicating that focusing retention efforts on these segments could yield substantial returns.
Overall Strategy Recommendations:
Nurture Champions: Implement loyalty programs and exclusive offers to further engage and retain our 'Champions'.
Re-engage At-Risk: Develop targeted campaigns for 'At Risk' RFM segments, focusing on improving engagement and addressing potential pain points.
Optimize High-Value Plans: Continue to enhance and market 'Enterprise' and 'Business' plans, as they are key drivers of long-term value and MRR.
Segment-Specific Retention: Tailor retention strategies based on the specific needs and churn drivers identified for each customer segment.
